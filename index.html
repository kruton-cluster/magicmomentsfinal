Founder: Chetan Gowda

Hey there, I’m Chetan Gowda, a wedding photographer with over a decade of experience. I absolutely love what I do, capturing the magic of a couple’s big day is my passion. I specialize in a mix of artistic and candid shots, showcasing the emotions and moments that make each wedding unique. My goal is to make my clients feel at ease in front of the camera so that I can capture their authentic selves.
